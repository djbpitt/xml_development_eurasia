Seal: شمس الامرا ۱۲۰۹

- (i) ل
    - ل is shorthand for باسم الله
- (ii) الهی
    - "Divine"
- (iii) بندگان حضرت
    - CS: "It is used for the king himself. This is the epithet for themselves."
- (iv) ۴
- (v - square seal): شمس الامرا ۱۲۰۹
- (1) بنايبان [= به نايبان]  حال و استقبال و دیسمکھان ديسپانديان و مقدمان و پتواريان پرگنه لاد   جالنه پور صوبه خجسته بنیاد اورنگ آباد
    - To all of the various officials of Lad Sankuy Pargana of the Sarkar of Jalna of Aurangabad.
    - dēsmuḵẖ: "A hereditary native offer under the former governments, exercising chief police and revenue authority over a district... entitled to the various fees and allowances, corresponding generally to the Zamindars of Bengal" (Wilson, 132). Possibly specific to Marathi regions.
    - dēspāndē: "The hereditary revenue accountant of a district or certain number of villages, holding office by hereditary tenure, and paid by lands... In some parts of Telingana the Despandya acts independently of the dēsmuḵẖ..." (Wilson, 132).
    - pat̥wārī (पटवारी): Mughal administrative term for one who keeps all the accounts connected with the lands of one or more villages
- (2) منصب قضاء پرگنه  بمشروط موازی بیست بیگه زمین خارج جمع  از سواد قصبه  و حق رسوم
    - The post of qazi of the aforementioned Pargana, which is accorded 20 Bighas excluded from the actual revenue receipt from the lands of that qasba...
    - khārij’jamʿ bikr: refers to a revenue receipt for a piece of territory
    - bikr: not a hindi word (बिकरी) but rather Persianised Arabic for "pure," in some other context also used for virgin
- (3) و لوازمات و غیره بموجب اسناد پیشین بنام عبد الرحیم مقرر و جاریست حالا نام برده منصب  بنام عبد یم پسر خود
    - ... and the the duties, etc., and on the basis of the previous sanads continued in the name of Abdur Rahim, (the aforementioned  qazi) and now he got transferred these rights in the name to his son, Abd al-Karim
- (4) مقرر ینده سند دار الصدور حاصل ساخته آمد لهذا از  هم منصب قضاء آنجا
    - and (Abd al-Karim) was endowed with the sanad of the Dār al-Sudūr.
    - Dār al-Sudūr
        - CK: "office of the sadar us sadur who was the competent authority to grant land for livelihood to the men of judiciary, religious establishement etc. see in Wilson, sadar us sadoor, even there is a book on the sadar us sadur of akbar’s period. Or see m. momin’s chancellory at the mughal court."
- (5) بدستور سابق بنام عبد یم  بحال داشته شد باید  مشار الیه بر حق رسوم خود
    - Therefore (li-hadhā), the post of qazi of that place should also be entrusted to the name of Abd al-Karim according to the former customs.
- (6) قابض و متصرف بوده در اجرای قضایا و اقامت جمعه و جماعات مامور بوده بدعای عمر و دولت 
    - The aforementioned (i.e. Abd al-Karim) should, according to his right, take possession and carry out the duties of the qazi, including the acting as Imam on the Friday prayers.
- (7) مشغول و موطف باشد درینباب سند معتبر دانسته حسب المسطور بعمل آرند
    - Certifying that the sanad is authentic, the order should be carried out
- marginalia: تحریر فی التاریخ يازدهم رتيع الاول  سنه ۱۲۵۹بيض>بيص 
    - *the year must be 1209 rather than 1259 since the seal is so clearly the former*
    - Last word byaz > short form of abyaz is the sign of authentication, some documents may have only letter ص usually in bold nib a different hand, it is from the competent authority or the approval authority on the recording of the detail by a waqai nawis on the basis of which a yaddasht or memorandum is scribed.

## А.Л. Кун, Заметка об изучении иранских наречий в Средней Азии (Ф33 О1 Д16)

Note:  This is an *extremely* rough draft, clearly repeatedly edited with lines crossed out, words added in in the margin, etc.  Original written in pre-reform Russian, transcription not.  Also, [[Zhukovskii's piece on the history of Iranian dialects|Zhukovskii, Kratkii ocherk istorii persidskogo iazykf i obzor iranskikh dialektov polosy goroda Isfagana (F17 O1 D11)]] makes a good companion piece.  I could make out so little of it that it is mostly useful just for indicating what debates Kun was participating in.

- Opens by noting that Iranian peoples have inhabited the Syr-Darya, Zaravshan, and Amu-Darya areas since the most ancient times (древнейший времени) (1).
- Like [[Zhukovskii|Zhukovskii, Kratkii ocherk istorii persidskogo iazykf i obzor iranskikh dialektov polosy goroda Isfagana (F17 O1 D11)]], he also references Dorn's work on Mazandaran; also work on the Kurds, Ossetians (1b).
- Kun engaged in his study of Iranian dialects in Turkestan during his service there (во времья той службы въ туркеснанском каре) (1b).
- He is interested in finding traces of words from ancient Sogd, Khorezm (4).

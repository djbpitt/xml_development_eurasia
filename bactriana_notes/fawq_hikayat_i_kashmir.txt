#### کشمیر جنّت نظیر

- According to the Hindu religion, the world is divided into four aeons (یگ): satya yuga, treta yuga, dwapar yuga, kali yuga.  Section continues discussing Hindu time cycles (3).
- Legend holds that the land at the base of the Himalayas was covered from the water for six Manvantaras (i.e. a time span equal to the life of Manu).  For this reasons, historians called it سرستی or ستی سر (p. 3).

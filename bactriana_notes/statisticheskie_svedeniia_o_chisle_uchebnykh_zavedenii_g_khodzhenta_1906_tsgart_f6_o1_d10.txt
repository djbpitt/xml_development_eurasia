Religion Statistics Вереоисповеданиям 1906 (p. 22)

- Православных: 617 in 1905, 476 in 1906
    - Note that broken down by gender there are fewer men but more women (women 118 - 131, men 499 - 345)
- Muslims: 17508 in 1905, 18099 in 1906
- Jews: 106 in 1905, 116 in 1906

Nationality (p. 26) is interesting; it lists 617 Russians (the exact same number as the Orthodox); for Uzbeks, Kirgiz, Sarts, and Tajiks, they are all lumped together without differentiation.  It does differentiate between Russian Jews (8) and local ones (106).  There are also 12 Armenians and an Iranian.

List of Ishans active in the city of Khujand (name, order, murids) (p. 80). There are 8 of them divided between the Хуфия and Чахрия orders.

- The Khufiya boast 220 murids total, the Chakhriya 230 (calculations mine).

The Mufti's Library: Islamic Sources Popular in Early Modern Transoxiana

@arabic @fatwas @primary_source

Being an attempt to get a handle on the Arabic texts (generally legal) that qazis and muftis would most frequently consult drawing on my primary sources and select secondary ones.

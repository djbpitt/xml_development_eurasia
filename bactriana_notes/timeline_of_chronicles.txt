#### Bukharan Chronicles Timeline

- 1747 Abd al-Rahman Tali: __Tarikh-i Abu’l Fayz Khan__ [P., Bukh.] 
    - Semenov translation, Tashkent 1959 (Storey-Bregel p. 1149, 1005) [[Tali, Istorii︠a︡ Abulfeiz-khana]]
- 1719-48 Mir M. Salim: __Silsilat al-salatin__ [P., Bukh.] * 
- 1745-68/9 M. Vafa Karminagi: __Tuhfat al-khani__ [P., Bukh.] * 
    - Excerpt translated into Russian (Storey Bregel p. 1150, 1007).
    - Four copies at al-Biruni: Semenov 1 p. 77, 195-199; 16, 2721, 2726/III, 1508, 615/III ; best quality is probably 2721 [[Muhammad Vifā-ī Karmīnagī, Tuhfat al-Khānī (al-Biruni, 2721)]]
    - [[Mirzoev|Mirzoev and A. M. Boldyrev, eds., Katalog vostochnykh rukopisei Akademii Nauk Tadzhikskoi SSR]] 1, p. 101; 105, 1426/1.
- 1785 M. Sadiq Jandari: __Dakhma-i shahan__ [P., Bukh.] * Janid rulers repenting 
- 1797 Mirza Badi Divan: __Majma al-arkam__ [P., Man.] 
    - Excerpt included in [[Levi Sela volume|Levi and Ron Sela, eds., Islamic Central Asia: An Anthology of Historical Sources]]
    - Used by: Bregel, The Administration of Bukhara Under the Manghits
    - According to Ilyor Karimov, A.B. Vil'danova has a full Russian translation of this.
- 1800 M. Sharif b. M. Naqi: __Taj al-tavarikh__ [P., Bukh.] *  [[Link to Notes|Mulla Muhammad Sharif, Taj al-Tawarikh (IVANRUz 2092)]]
    - "Автор, между прочим, останавливается и на имевших в описаемый им период народных волнениях, а также (лл. 295 и сл,) на инсценированном Надир-шахом (1148/1736-1160/1747) "съезде мусульманских ученых для согласования шиитского и суннитского пероучения"... Освещение этого события с точки зрения бухарских ученых является, сесомненно, ценным дополнением к той оценке, которое это событие получило со стороны подчиненных османской империи багдадских ученых и со стороны персидских писателей" (Semenov 1, 85).
    - Mirzoev 1, p. 105; 109, 2282.
    - Used by: Bregel, The Administration of Bukhara Under the Manghits
- 1818 Abd al-Karim Bukhari: __Afghan va Kabul__... [P., Bukh.] 
    - Transcribed by Charles Schefer, 1873.
- 1822 Fazli Namangani: __Umar-nama__ [P., Ferg.]*
- 1822 Mushrif Isfaragi: __Shah-nama-i Umar-khani__ [P., Ferg.]* 
- 1826 “Mu’in”: __Tarikh-i ava’il i avakhir__ [P., Bukh.]
    - Mirzoev 1, p. 96; 100, 629.
- 1778-1829 Munis historian of Khiva [& Agahi, Firdaws al-iqbal, T., 1805-42]
- 1826 __Tarikh-i Amir Haidar__ [P., Bukh.]* 
    - Semenov has a full translation of this work - but unpublished and probably in Dushanbe (Semenov 1, 86).
- 1824-30 M. Ya’qub: __Gulshan al-muluk__; (1828-30) __Risalah__ [P., Bukh.] * 
    - (Gulshan) Mirzoev 1, p. 104; 108, 2663.
    - [[Risāla-i Muḥammad Yaʿqūb Bukhārī]]
    - [[Notes on a Petersburg copy|Muhammad Yusuf Munshi, Tarikh-i Muqim Khan and Gulshan al-Muluk (V 681)]]
- 1830s Husayn b. Shah Murad: __Mahazin al-taqwa__ [P., Bukh.]* 
- 1830s Mir Alim Bukhari: __Fath-nama-i sultani__ [P., Bukh.] [[Link to notes|Risāla-i Muḥammad Yaʿqūb Bukhārī]]
    - Semenov 1, p. 87; 218, 1838.
    - Author was ordered to produce this work by Mhammad Alim-bek to glorify his polity.  Covers the deeds of Shah-Murad, Haidar, and Nasrulla (Semenov 1, 87).
- 1842-3 M. Hakim Khan: __Muntakhab al-Tavārīkh__ [P., Ferg] [[Link to notes|Muḥammad Ḥakīm Khān, Muntakhab al-Tavārīkh 1842]]
    - Semenov 1, p. 88; 219, 592
    - This work is by the son of Sa'id Mas'um Khan and focuses on Kokand, but is a general work as well.  "Кроме того, труд заключает описание переживаний и приключений самого автора во время его скитаний по России, Ирану и Бухаре" (Semenov 1, 88).
    - In addition to a Dushanbe facsimile, there seems to be an edited edition (though it was in a display case and I could not look inside): Muhammad Hakim Khan, Muntakhab al-Tawarikh, Selected History, Research Institute for Languages and Cultures of Asia and Africa, ILCAA 2009.  [[Link|Muḥammad Ḥakīm Khān, Muntakhab al-Tavārīkh 1842]]
- 1845 Shigavul: __Tarikh-i Ali Quli__
- 1860 Mirza Shams Bukhari: __Bayan-i ba’d-i khawadisat-i Bukhara__ [P., Bukh.] 
    - Translation: V.V. Grigor'evyi, O nekotorykh" sobytiiakh" v" Bukhare, Kokande i Kashgare (Kazan: Universitetskaia tipografiia, 1861).  Alisher Navoi Redkii Fond: ПУ-10597.
    - A nice Tehrani edition: [[Shams Bukhārāyī, Tārīkh-i Bukhārā, Khūqand va Kāshghar]], ed. Muḥammad Akbar ʻAshīq (Tehran: Āyinah-ʾi Mīrās̲, 1998).
- 1860-1869 [[Mīr Salmān Samarqandī, Tafsīl wa Bayān-i Dawlat-i Jamāʿat-i Manghit (IVRSP C 667)]]
- 1867-8 Avaz M. Attar: __Tuhfat al-tavarikh-i khani__ [P., Ferg.]* 
- 1860s Thana’i: __Tavarikh-i khorezm shahi__ [T.] 
- 1871-2 Niyaz M. b. Ashur M. Khoqandi: __Tavarikh-i Shahrukhiyya__ [P., Ferg.] 
- 1871-2 [[Dhikr-i Tārīkh-i Abū al-Fayḍ Khān (Anonymous) (IVRSP C 667)]]
- 1862-87 M. Salih Khoja Tashkandi: __Tarikh-i Jadida-ye Tashkent__ [P., Ferg.]
    - Unpublished, but partially transcribed by Babajanov: http://zerrspiegel.orientphil.uni-halle.de/t386.html
- 1895-1897 Ahmad Danish (Ahmad b. Mir Nasir b. Yusuf al-Hanafi al-Sidiqi al-Bukhari): __Tarjimat al-ahwal-i amiran-i Bukhara-i sharif az amir Daniyal ta 'asr-i amir 'Abd al-Ahad__
    - Iusupova p. 214; 586, 1987.  Iusupova has a detailed biography of the author.
    - ff. 22a-23b, 29a-b describes the uprising of madrassa students against the Amir (Babajanov, "Foreword," 18).
    - Translation: И.А. Наджафовой, "Трактат Ахмад Даниша **История Мангытысой династии** Dushanbe, 1967.
    - Mirzoev 1 p. 109; 113, 572. 
- late 1800s [[Tarikh-i Muhammad Sidiq Hashmat (al-Biruni 2663/III)]]
- early 1900s Alim Khoja b. Mirza Sayyid Khoja Mushrif Bukharai: __Ta'rikh-i Amiran-i Manghit-i Bukhara__
    - A general history of the late Manghit period.
    - Mirzoev 1, p. 120; 123, 2354/IV.  "... о намерении царское правительства ввести в Бухаре руссукие законы (زکون)..." (p. 121).  
- 1901/1906-7 Abd al-Azim Sami: __Tuhfa-i shahi__ [P., Bukh]*; __Tarikh-i salatin-i manghitiyya__ [P., Bukh.]
    - (Tarikh-i) Mirzoev 1, p. 116; 120, 786/II.
    - Tuhfa-i shahi: Semenov 1, p. 92; 236, 1458/II
    - Dissertation housed at the library next to al-Biruni has Russian translation of the latter: L.M. Epifanova, Neofitsial'naia versiia istorii Bukhary Mirzy 'Abdalazima Sami 'Ta'rikh-i salatin-i mangitiia dar as-saltana-i Bukhara-i Sharif.  Dissertation, Akademia nauk Uzbekskoi SSR, Institut Vostokovedeniia, Tashkent, 1959.
- 1885-1910 Muhammad Sadiq Khoja Gulshani 'Abdul'l Ahad: __Tarikh-i Humayun-i Gulshani__.  [[link to notes|Tārīkh-i Humāyūn-i Gulshanī, 1328]]
    - Institute of Oriental Studies and Written Heritage in Dushanbe, the late Amriyazdon Alimardonov was working on publishing it.  (Ref. Gross - see note).
- 1911-14 M. Yusuf Beg Bayani: __Shajara-i khorezm shahi__ [T., Khorezm] 
- 1920 M. Salim Bek “Salimi”: __Tarikh-i Salimi__ [P., Bukh.]
    - Semenov 1, p. 93: 237, 2016.
    - Used by: Wennberg, An Inquiry into Bukharan Qadimism
    - Full translation: N.K. Norkulov, “'Tar'ikh-i Salimi' - Tsennyi istochnik po istorii Bukharskogo emirata 1860-1920 gg.” (Tashkent: Akademiia Nauk Uzbekskoi SSR, Institut Vostokovedeniia, 1968).
- 1923-27 Muḥammad  ʿAlī  ibn-i Muḥammad  Sayyid  Baljuwānī,  Taʾrīkh-i  Nāfīʾī,  ed.  Ahror Muxtorov (Dushanbe, 1994) in the Arabic script.
    - Used by: Fragner, “Traces of Modernisation and Westernisation? Some Comparative Considerations concerning Late Bukhāran Chronicles" (2005).
- Sharif-jan Makhdum-i Sadr-i Ziya.  __Ruznamih__
    - Used by: Dudoignon, “Faction struggles among the Bukharan ulama during the colonial, the revolutionary and the early Soviet periods (1826-1929)
    - Partial translation: Sharīf Jān Makhdūm [[Ṣadr Z̤iyāʾ et al., The Personal History of a Bukharan Intellectual]] (Leiden: Brill, 2004).
- [[Ṣadr Żīāʾ, Tārīkh-i Salāṭīn ʿUthmānī va Nasab-i Ishān (al-Biruni 1304-II)]]
    - Inappropriately named - in fact covers Transoxiana, Iran, Afghanistan, and Russia
- 1931-1932: Hamid Bakaef: __Muntakhab-i Ta'rikh-i Amiran-i Manghit__
    - Mirzoev 1, p. 123; 124, 1329.  "... подробно описывает кровавые события в Самарканде 1285/1868 г. в резултате возмущения части реакцигнною духовенства и учащихся медресе..." (p. 123).

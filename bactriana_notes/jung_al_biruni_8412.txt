Jung (al-Biruni 8412)

@tash_biruni @notebookjung @fiqh @khorezm @primary_source

[[Jung Literature from al-Biruni Card Catalog]]

This jung is of the fiqh genre - i.e. like [[10922|Jūng-i Fatāwī wa Riwāyāt wa Masāyil-i Fiqhīya (al-Biruni 10992)]], for instance, it is composed almost exclusively of Arabic-language excerpts from fiqh manuals.  Because of a Turki inscription dating to 1340 on the back inside cover, Saidakbar believes it to be a Khorezmian jung.

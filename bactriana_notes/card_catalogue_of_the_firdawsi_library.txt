#### Printed Works
تذکرة الشعرای عبد الله خواجه عبدی (No. 10130) [[Link to Notes|Taẕkirat al-Shuʿarā-ī ʿAbdallah Khwājah ʿAbdī, 1904]]

- From Энциклопедияи Советии Тоҷик (Vol. 7, 1987): 
    - Written by Adullahkhojai Abdi (1870-1922) in 1904.  Covers 118 individuals, mostly Bukharan intellectuals, but also a few Iranians and Afghans (p. 229).
    - Published 1983 in Dushanbe.

تذکرة الشعرای محترم (No. 8348, 8339, 8338, 8337)

- Author: محترم حاجی شعمت الله
- Dushanbe, 1975.
- From Энциклопедияи Советии Тоҷик (Vol. 7, 1987): 
    - Written in 1908. Includes poets from the 19th and early 20th centuries.  There are two copies, the author's own draft in Tajikistan (which has been edited and published) and one copied by Sadr Zia in Uzbekistan.  Not completed.  The Zia version has an additional conclusion.

تذکار اشعار (No. 13449) [[Link to Notes|Ṣadr Żīāʾ, Taẕkār-i Ashʿār]]

- Author: Sadr Zia شریفجان مخدوم صدر ضیاء
- Published 1380
- From Энциклопедияи Советии Тоҷик (Vol. 7, 1987): 
    - Written in 1905-07.  Accounts here differ somewhat from those of Afzal-Makhdum, Abdi, and Pirmasti

تحبة الاحباب فی تذکرة الاصحاب (No. 8717, 8783, 8784) [[Link to manuscript notes|Vażiḥ, Tuḥfat al-Aḥbāb fi Taẕkirat al-Āṣḥāb]]

## Сборник судебных постановлений 17-18 в. (PNB F924 D624). 106 листов.  17-18в., Средняя Азия

From the Fond Opis: Сборник судебных постановлений и выписок из сочинений по мусульманскому законоведению.  На перс. и араб. яз.  Текст: выписки размещены на листах отдельно друг от друга, иногда текст наклонен, занимает почти все поле листа...

General Notes:

- Scanned pages: 92b-93 (a fatwa); 96b-97 (cases)
- There are occasionally section headings written in red (e.g. کتاب الطهاره), just as in [[delo 558|Sbornik vypisok iz sochinenii po fikkhu i iuridicheskikh kazusov (PNB F924 D558)]] (though absent a ToC), but generally the cases here seem to be organized more haphazardly.
    - It almost seems as though there was some initial attempt to organize the book thematically, but then the cases ended up just being heaped together - possibly even by a different author, as the handwriting seems to change.
- Just like as in [[delo 558|Sbornik vypisok iz sochinenii po fikkhu i iuridicheskikh kazusov (PNB F924 D558)]], this author quotes شرح ملا شمس الدین محمد all the time to justify his fatwas.  (See, for instance, p. 85 where he does so twice).
- Some evidence that the text is Bukharan in origin: درین مسئله که مسماة عایشه زوجۀ خالد که ساکنة در ولایت بخارا است (p. 38b).  Note that the same case references Balkh as well.
    - Reference to ولایت ابوت or possibly ابوق (p. 41).
- Stray references to works used to justify rulings: خزانة الاوقعات

Inconsistent Index (*mine, not the author's*)

- 65-66: کتاب الصوم
- 65- 68b: کتاب کرهات; *following the word کرهات it continues*: از جونگی مرزا [میرزا] شریف بخاری [نوّن *few cases - mostly excerpts and commentary*
- 69-72: کتاب الذبایح *few cases - mostly excerpts and commentary*
- 72b-73: کتاب الاضحیه *few cases - mostly excerpts and commentary*
- 73- 78: کتاب الصید
- From p. 79 different handwriting begins and the continuity of the previous sections is lost; also possible that some pages were lost here.

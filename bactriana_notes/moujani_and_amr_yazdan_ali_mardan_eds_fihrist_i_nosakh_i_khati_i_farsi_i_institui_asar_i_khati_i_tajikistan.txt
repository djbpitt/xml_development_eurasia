## Saīd ʻAlī Mūjānī and Amr Yazdān ʻAlī Mardān, eds., Fihrist-i nusakh-i khaṭṭī-i Fārsī-i Anstītū-yi Ās̲ār-i Khaṭṭī-i Tājīkistān, 4 vols. (Tehran: Vizārat-i Umūr-i Khārijah, 1997).

#### Volume 1

تحفه الاحباب فی تذکره الاصحاب p. 112; 483 [[Link to Notes|Vażiḥ, Tuḥfat al-Aḥbāb fi Taẕkirat al-Āṣḥāb]]
قاری رحمت الله ابن عاشور محمد البخاری متخلص به واضح

- Author lived 1817 - 1893.  Book written 1864-1870.
- This is a poetical biography / excerpts type text.  Interesting because it includes Iranian and Central Asian authors.  It also includes information on a circle of Khoqandi poets.
- From this page forward there are lots of other poetical biographies as well - tons of em.
- About Tajik poets in the late 18th and 19th century.

تذکره p. 112; 952
ملّا عبدالمطلب خواجه فهمی

- 1860.
- شامل شرح حال یک عدّه شاعران معروف و بعضی @@عالمان معاصر مولف@@ - so this also includes non-poets.
- Has a brief history of some of the amirs of Bukhara and the end of the work.

تذکره الشعرا p. 113; 394
حاجی نعمت الله ولد قاضی شرف الدین متخلص به محترم

- Author died 1920; finished in 1910.
- Includes 125 poets; not completed - only includes poets alif - qaf.

تذکره الشعرا یا تذکرۀ عبدی p. 115; 3220
میر عبدالله خواجه ابن مختار خواجه عبدی

- Died 1922; completed 1904
- Entries on 118 poets from the second half of the 19th century and beginning of the 20th.
- The original is at al-Biruni - number 64 there.

تذکرۀ منظومه p. 115; 230/1
قاضی محمد شریف جان مخدوم صدر متخلص به ضیاء

- So this is one of Sadr Zia's literary productions, finished in 1910.
- Focuses on 45 poets and literateurs in the second half of the 19th century and beginning of the 20th.

مجمع الشعرا p. 116; 1265
فضلی نمنگانی

- Author died 1822; written 1812.
- Very brief summary of the lives of Kokandi poets in both Tajik and Uzbek.

## Mīr Sayyid Qamar Sudūr

## Jung-i Qāḍī Mīr Sayyid Qamar Ṣudūr, ms. IVANUz no. 2588, f.

Author: قاضی میر سید قمر صدور
Broadest date range listed: 1261- 1303 (1840s-1850s)

- Described in [[Gulomov, "O nekotorykh podlinnykh dokumentakh iz kollektsii rukopisnykh proizvedenii fonda Instituta Vostokovedeniia AN RUz"]]
    - Inventory number 2588: astrological writings, divination. Also 151 rivayat. This collection was the personal archive of Qazi Sayyid Kamara (5). Also has some documents appointing a mudarris and qazi (6).
    - Massive notebook of various rivayats, rescripts, astrological scribblings, etc.

- Histoire General des Voyages ou Nouvelle Collection de Toutes les Relations de Voyages Parmeret Par Terre (Paris, 1749.  Alisher Navoi Redkii Fond: Пи 3155 9.

    - Alisher Navoi Redkii Fond: Пу 1980 2.

    - Visited Bukhara with a merchant caravan; fluent in Uzbek.  Unpublished description in ЦГВИА, ф, 67, No. 103  (Muminov, 12).

    - Alisher Navoi Redkii Fond: Пу 8493
    - Naturalist Leman who wrote a great deal about education in Bukhara (Muminov, 12).

    - Alisher Navoi Redkii Fond: Пу 1716
- G.A. Arendarenko, 1880.  G.A. Arendarenko, Bukhara i Afganistan v nachale 80-kh godov XIX veka (zhurnaly komandirovok G.A. Arendarenko), ed. B.G. Gafurov (Moscow: Izd. "Nauka" Glavnaia redaktsiia vostochnoi literatury, 1974).
    - Alisher Navoi Redkii Fond: Пя 11473.
    - Sent on a political mission by Kaufman and related to British encroachment.
- V.P. Nalivkin, 1880s-1910s.  Many publications, most about Kokand / Fergana.
- N.P. Ostroumov, 18802-1910s.  More of an Islamoved than a traveler.

    - More of a naturalist, really, but might have some ethnographic notes anyway.
- Logofet, 1911(?).  D. Logofet, Bukharskoe khanstvo pod russkim protektoratom, 1911.  *Many other publications as well.*

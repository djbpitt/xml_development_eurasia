This is the orientalist who wrote about the Juybari Shaykhs.  He also appears to have been intimately involved in Soviet regulation of waqfs, so this would be a useful source in that regard.

Biography: Pavel Petrovich Ivanov (1843-1942) - vydaiushchiisia istorik Srednei Azii

- Finished school (Туркестанская учительская семинария в Ташкенте) in 1914; then worked as a teacher in a Russian-Kirgiz school in Chimkent.
- 1916 conscripted to fight in WWI.
- After the war, taught in Tashkent and Frunze (Bishkek).

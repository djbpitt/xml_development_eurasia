## Golib Negmatovich Kurbanov, “Pechati Bukharskogo khanstva XIX - nachala XX vekov” (Moscow: Akademiia Nauk SSSR, Institut Vostokovedeniia, 1990).

This 389 page dissertation is about chancellery seals on official documents and how to deal with them.  The author examines the stamps themselves as well as their imprint on various documents (18-19).

References

- M. Talibova, Kh. Turaev.  Iz opyta registratsii kollektsii dokumentov Bukharskogo muzeia.  Bartol'dskie chteniia.  1984.

Background on archives:

- The formation of the archive of the Qushbegi (И-126 at ЦГА) began under Shah Murad (1785-1800) and went through Alim Khan (1910-1920) (19).
- All materials were transferred from the Bukharan Museum to Tashkent in 1937-38 (20).
- The designation of the Waqf fond at ЦГА is И-323.  "Грамоты разиовременные, значительная часть относится к изучаемому периоду" (20) - i.e. 19th - beginning 20th century.

Document Descriptions

- Example of Yarliq dealing directly with madrasa cells: Б.М.З. инв. но. 350/273.  1917.
- There are loads of "kupchaia" in the Bukharan museum, i.e. deeds of sale.
- There are also marriage documents.  *Possible to track new residents to Bukhara this way?*
- "Letters" genre:
    - Б.М.З. инв. но. 509/424: A mulla / mudarris writes the following - "...  о недовольстве падением нравов и требовнием принять меры" (367).
    - Б.М.З. инв. но. 9759/12: "Предпрсание казы ал-куззама Бако ходжи учащимся медресе, бухара, даты нет" (368).  (Katolog 1, No. 119, 269).

## Muḥammad Muṭīʿa al-Ḥāfiẓ, Nizār ʾAbaẓah, ʿUlamāʾ Dimashq wa ʾAʿīānuhā fī al-Qarn al-Thalith ʿAshar al-Hijrī, Part 3: 1304 - 1360 h. / 1886 - 1844 m., Dār al-Fikr, Damascus

*This is a biographical dictionary from which I only copied a single entry while I was in Damascus.*

 __Abd al-Rasul al-Bukharli عبد الرسول البخارلي__ *the **l** in Bukharli is not a typo, at least on my part*

- Life: ? - death in 1297 / 1879
- Identifies Bukharli as عالم صوفي as well as Naqshbandi and Hanafi in his full name and title: عبد الرسول البخارلي الحنفي النقشبندي
- Immigrated from Bukhara to Damascus where he was accepted by commmoners and nobles alike (الخواصّ و العوام).
- Bukharli's qualities: أديب كاملل, آمر بالمعروف, ناهٍ عن المنكر, ما جلس في مجلس إلا وعظ و ذكّر, جواد كريم, كثير الوداد, لا يترك زيارة إخوانه, و له كرامات ظاهرة, ما رآه امرؤ أحبه
- Exact date of death: نهار الأربعاء 25 ذي القعدة 1297
    - Buried in مقبرة الدحداح

*Not much information here; interesting that it remarks specifically that he did not engage in frivolous activities and only met with people to spread the word of Islam*

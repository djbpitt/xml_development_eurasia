## Jūng-i Masāʾil wa Wāqiʿāt (al-Biruni 8887) 

General remarks:

- Card catalog has the author as میر حبیب الله بن قاضی عبد جلال الحسینی *but I don't see that reference yet*
- A much tidier work than many of the other jungs.  Also wider pages.
- Organized by fiqh topic, composed of issues / questions (masala), a short Persian sentence or two, and Arabic references.

Letter magic (inside front cover)

- (1) من كتب هذه الحروف على كتابه فيكون قادراً على مسأله
- (2) ح ه ح لا ا ا ه ا لا ا ح     ح و ح لااا ه االسکح
    - *note: a circle around the second of the two series of letters in both rows 2 and 3*
- (3) ح و ح لال ع ااا ب ح     رواح لا ااه ااا ح

## Fond 933. Tiurkskie rukopisi. Novaia seriia. Opis' fonda.  Leningrad, 1985.  Gosudarstvennaia Ordena Trudovogo Krasnogo Znameni Publichnaia Biblioteka im. M. E. Caltykova-Shedrina.  Otdel rukopisei

Many of the contents of this opis are 19th century Turkchi translations of Persian works.  Here I am merely jotting down some notes to get a sense of which works were being translated. I didn't record the individual volumes, but there is loads of Alisher Navoi.  Translations directly from Arabic or Turkish to Uzbek are fewer, but not absent.  293 entries total.

*Note: the original opis uses Cyrillic diacriticals, but instead I doubled vowels to indicate a long vowel.  I have not yet to determine if the author indicates the translator or the original.*
